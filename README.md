# GeoLens

**GeoLens** is a modern desktop application that allows users to precisely pinpoint the location of their photos based on EXIF metadata and visualize them on an interactive map.

## ï¿½ Description

GeoLens transforms your photo collection into an interactive journey. By analyzing the hidden GPS metadata embedded in your images, it automatically places your memories on a detailed world map. 

Whether you're a professional photographer tracking shoot locations, a traveler documenting your adventures, or simply someone organizing family albums, GeoLens provides a visual way to explore your history. It not only shows you *where* you've been but also allows you to generate heatmaps of your activity and export beautiful PDF travel logs to share with others.
