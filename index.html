Powerful Features

Everything you need to discover the story behind your photos.

GPS Extraction

Automatically extract GPS coordinates from your photos' EXIF data and visualize them on an interactive map.

Smart Analysis

No GPS? No problem. Our system scans your photo for landmarks, signs, and vegetation to estimate the location.

Heatmap

Visualize your travel density with a dynamic heatmap that highlights your most visited areas.

Stats & Export

Track countries visited, miles traveled, export your journey to PDF, GPX, or KML.
